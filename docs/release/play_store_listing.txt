Simple arcade reflex game — tap to stack blocks and beat your high score.

Full description (plain text, max 4000 chars):
StackTap is a minimal, addictive arcade game where you tap the screen to drop moving blocks and stack them as high as possible.

- Tap to drop the moving block.
- If the block overlaps the previous one, it trims to the overlapping area and the stack grows.
- Miss the overlap and the run ends — try again to beat your high score.

Features:
- Quick, replayable sessions
- Simple one‑tap controls
- Color progression that changes as you stack
- Local high score tracking (no accounts, no analytics)

Privacy & Data:
All game data (high score and basic settings) are stored locally on the device. We do not collect or transmit personal data.

Call to action:
Tap to start stacking — can you reach the sky?
